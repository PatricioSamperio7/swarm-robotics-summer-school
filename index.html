Technical Roadmap

Week 1: Simulation

Focus on ROS 2 architecture, distributed communication (DDS/QoS), and large-scale Gazebo simulations.

Week 2: Physical Swarm

Hardware-in-the-loop integration, cooperative control algorithms, and deployment on real robotic agents.

Venue: Monterrey, Mexico

Why Monterrey?

Monterrey is Mexico's premier industrial ecosystem and manufacturing hub. As a center for technological innovation, FIME UANL provides the ideal infrastructure for advanced robotics development and international networking.

FAQ

Is prior ROS 2 experience required?

Not mandatory, but basic programming is needed.

Is hardware mandatory?

Only your laptop. Robotic platforms are provided.

Is the program hybrid?

Yes. Week 1 is Online; Week 2 is On-site at FIME.

Language of instruction?

Primarily Technical English.
