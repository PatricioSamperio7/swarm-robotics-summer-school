Swarm Robotics Summer School 2026

Design and Implementation of Swarm Robotic Systems using ROS 2

Practitioner Track Technical Education Program

Faculty of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering (FIME)
Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León — Monterrey, Mexico

Program Overview

This Summer School focuses on the practical design, implementation, and validation of distributed swarm robotic systems using ROS 2, cooperative control algorithms, and IoT-edge integration. Participants will develop a fully functional swarm robotic system addressing real-world engineering challenges.

Real Engineering Challenges

Industrial Infrastructure Cooperative Inspection
Collaborative Load Transportation
Distributed Exploration and Mapping
IoT Environmental Monitoring using Mobile Nodes

Technical Topics

Swarm Robotics Fundamentals
ROS 2 Distributed Architecture
DDS Communication and QoS
IoT and Edge Computing
Embedded Systems Integration
Cooperative Control Algorithms

Format
