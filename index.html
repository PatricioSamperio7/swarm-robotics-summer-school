Why Attend?

Value Proposition

  • Hands-on multi-robot coordination.

  • Hands-on practitioner certification focused on real engineering deployment.

Who Should Apply?

Academic Program Depth

Advanced Core Modules

  • Swarm Robotics Foundations & Emergent Behavior Analysis.
  • ROS 2 Scalable Namespace Architectures.
  • DDS/QoS Communication: Failure Tolerance & Latency Handling.
  • Hardware-Software Integration Workflows.
  • Distributed Decision-making Systems.
  • Edge Computing Integration for Robotic Swarms.
  • IoT Data Acquisition using Mobile Robotic Nodes.

Real Engineering Challenges

  • Cooperative Industrial Infrastructure Inspection.

  • Cooperative Object Transport.
  • IoT Environmental Monitoring using Robotic Agents.

Application Process

Eligibility & Selection

Selection based on technical profile and motivation:

  • Motivation statement quality.
  • Programming experience.
  • Academic interest in Swarm Robotics.
  • Limited capacity cohort (approx. 20–25 participants) to ensure hands-on mentoring.

Technical Recommendations

  • Ubuntu 22.04 LTS recommended: Provides optimal ROS 2 Humble/Jazzy compatibility for development.
  • C++/Python: Foundational knowledge in OOP is recommended.

FAQ

Do participants receive IEEE certification?

Yes, an official participation certificate is issued by the IEEE RAS Student Chapter.

Is hardware mandatory?

Only your laptop is required. Robotic platforms are provided by the lab.

Is accommodation included?

No, participants must arrange their own lodging in Monterrey.

Are international students accepted?

Yes, we welcome students and researchers from all countries.
