Program Depth

Core Academic Modules

01 Foundations

Swarm Robotics Theory, bio-inspired algorithms, and self-organization principles.

02 Architecture

ROS 2 Distributed Architectures, namespaces, and node lifecycle for multi-agent systems.

03 Communication

DDS Middleware, Quality of Service (QoS) tuning, and robot-to-robot communication.

04 Hardware

Embedded systems integration, sensor fusion, and physical hardware deployment.

05 Control

Cooperative control algorithms, decentralized decision making, and emergent behavior.

FAQ

Do participants receive IEEE certification?

Yes, all participants who complete the 25-hour program and final demonstration will receive an official certificate.

Is accommodation included?

No, participants must arrange their own lodging in Monterrey. We can provide a list of nearby recommendations.

Are international students accepted?

Absolutely. We encourage applications from students and researchers worldwide.

Is prior ROS 2 experience required?

While helpful, Week 1 is designed to provide the necessary foundations for simulation and communication.
