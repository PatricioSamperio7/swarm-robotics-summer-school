Swarm Robotics Summer School 2026

Design and Implementation of Distributed Robotic Swarms using ROS 2. Hosted at FIME — Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León.

About the Program

This practitioner-focused summer school explores swarm robotics, distributed autonomy, ROS 2 architectures, IoT integration and edge computing. Participants will design cooperative robotic systems solving real-world industrial challenges.

Program Structure

  • Week 1 — Intensive Technical Training
  • Swarm Robotics Fundamentals
  • ROS 2 Distributed Architectures
  • IoT and Edge Computing
  • Hardware Integration
  • Cooperative Control Algorithms
  • Week 2 — Applied Project Development
