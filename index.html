Swarm Robotics Summer School 2026

Design and Implementation of Swarm Robotic Systems using ROS 2. Practitioner Track — Distributed Intelligence, IoT Integration and Cooperative Robotics. Hosted at FIME — UANL, Mexico.

About the Summer School

This practitioner-focused robotics program explores distributed autonomy, multi-robot coordination and real-world swarm robotics applications. Participants will design scalable robotic systems integrating ROS 2, edge computing and cooperative intelligence.

Program Highlights

Week 1

Intensive technical formation: ROS 2 architecture, swarm algorithms, IoT communication and hardware integration.

Week 2

Applied team project solving real-world problems: inspection, mapping and cooperative robotics.

Final Demonstration

Live technical presentation and system validation. Documentation and technical review included.

Venue

Faculty of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering (FIME) Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León Monterrey, Mexico.

Organized By

IEEE Robotics and Automation Society Student Branch. Contact: swarmrobotics@uanl.mx
