An intensive technical program focused on collective emergent behavior, distributed control, and real-world deployment of robotic swarms. From industrial inspection to cooperative logistics.

Course Syllabus

Week 1: Intensive Technical Training

MODULES 1-5

  • Introduction to Swarm Robotics & Complex Systems
  • Self-organization principles & Emergent Behavior
  • Advanced ROS 2 Distributed Architectures
  • Multi-robot Namespaces and Scalability
  • DDS, Quality of Service (QoS) & Latency Control
  • Robot-to-Robot (R2R) Communication Protocols
  • IoT & Edge Computing for Robotic Swarms
  • Embedded Systems & Hardware Integration with ROS 2
  • Cooperative Control Algorithms & Local Rules
  • Analysis of Distributed Decision Making

Week 2: Applied Project Development

CHALLENGE TRACKS

  • Track 1: Cooperative Industrial Infrastructure Inspection
  • Track 2: Cooperative Transport of Light Loads
  • Track 3: Distributed Exploration & Swarm Mapping
  • Track 4: Distributed IoT Environmental Monitoring
  • System Design & Technical Planning
  • Distributed Swarm Behavior Implementation
  • Testing, Debugging & Behavior Validation
  • Final Demonstration & Technical Presentation
